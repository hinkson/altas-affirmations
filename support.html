Quick answers

Where are my custom affirmations stored?
They are stored locally on your device and shared locally with the widget extension when needed.
Does the app require an account?
No. Alta's Affirmations is designed without accounts, analytics, ads, or tracking.
Why does the composer limit affirmation length?
Shorter lines are easier to read in lock screen widgets, so the composer helps keep text widget-friendly.
Can I control bold-language affirmations?
Yes. Bold-language variants stay hidden unless you enable them, and you can choose which words are allowed.
