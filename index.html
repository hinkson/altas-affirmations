What it does

Small reminders with a personal center.

Custom-first affirmations

Write the exact line you need and save it locally in the category where it belongs.

Lock screen guidance

Character counts and readiness notes help each affirmation fit comfortably in a widget.

Affirmation packs

Everyday categories and tender life-event packs are ready on day one, with 30 built-in lines each.

Love-note corner

Add an optional sweetheart name so the app can hold a little pocket of personal reassurance.

Opt-in bold language

Savager lines stay off until enabled, with word-by-word control for what can appear.

Widget rotation

Choose which categories feed the widget and whether reminders stay fixed or refresh over time.

Tone

Gentle when you need gentle. Punchy when you ask for punchy.

Privacy

Your words stay yours.

Alta's Affirmations is designed without accounts, ads, analytics, or tracking. Custom affirmations and settings are stored on your device and shared locally with the widget extension.
